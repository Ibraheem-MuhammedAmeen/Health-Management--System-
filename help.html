    The Personal Health Assistant is designed to provide health-related information, recommendations, and assistance. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult a healthcare professional for any medical concerns or emergencies.

    When using the application, you may be required to provide personal information. We take your privacy seriously and have implemented security measures to protect your data. Review our Privacy Policy to understand how your information is collected, used, and stored.

    While the application strives to provide accurate and up-to-date information, it's important to verify any health-related advice or recommendations with a qualified healthcare provider.

    You are responsible for the information you provide in the application. Make sure to provide accurate and honest details about your health and symptoms for the best recommendations.

    The application can offer general health advice, but it is not a substitute for professional medical diagnosis or treatment. If you have specific health concerns, consult a qualified healthcare provider.

    In case of a medical emergency, dial emergency services or seek immediate medical attention. The application is not equipped to handle emergencies.
